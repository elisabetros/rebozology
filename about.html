Rebozology is a gift from Mexico to the world of motherhood.

“ Rebozology is the online platform where you can learn about the rebozo uses and techniques from expert Mexican childbirth professionals and where you can buy rebozos directly from Mexican artisans, keeping alive their traditions so they are able to support their families. 

  Rebozology are 100% Mexican made so every time you buy our products you are contributing to support indigenous Mexican families.

Meet Adriana

Rebozology was founded by Adriana, a Mexican woman who gave birth in Denmark and who experienced the benefits of the traditional rebozo techniques during pregnancy, labor and the postpartum period. She also continued the use of rebozo as a baby carrier and enjoyed all the benefits from it. Adriana decided to make this tool available for every mother and birth professional around the world and at the same time to support local indigenous communities in Mexico where rebozos are produced. 

Adriana is married to a Dane and has lived in Copenhagen since 2012. They have two beautiful and sweet kids born in 2014 and 2017. She loves photography and travelling.
